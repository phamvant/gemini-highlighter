# Privacy Policy for Gemini Highlighter

Last updated: May 24, 2026

Gemini Highlighter helps you study articles by copying selected article text into a Gemini prompt, opening Gemini, and restoring source-linked highlights.

## Data handled by the extension

The extension handles the selected article text you choose, nearby text context used to restore highlights, the source page URL, Gemini conversation links, and local session identifiers.

## How data is stored

This data is stored locally in Chrome extension storage on your device. Gemini Highlighter does not operate a server and does not transmit this data to the developer.

## Clipboard and Gemini

When you use the Ask action, Gemini Highlighter writes the prompt text to your clipboard and opens Gemini. You decide whether to paste or submit that text to Gemini. Gemini is a Google service and is governed by Google's own terms and privacy policies.

## Data sharing and sale

Gemini Highlighter does not sell your data. The extension does not share your data with the developer, advertisers, analytics providers, or other third parties.

## Data deletion

You can delete saved highlight sessions from the page controls provided by the extension. You can also remove all extension data by uninstalling Gemini Highlighter or clearing the extension's site data in Chrome.

## Contact

For privacy questions, use the support contact listed on the Chrome Web Store listing for this extension.
